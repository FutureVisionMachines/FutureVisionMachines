About FutureVisionMachines

Making enterprise technology accessible to everyone

Our Story

FutureVisionMachines was founded with a single belief: powerful software shouldn't be restricted by cost or complexity.

Too many small and medium businesses struggle with manual processes, fragmented systems, and expensive "enterprise" tools that don't fit their needs. We exist to change that.

Based in Edmonton, Alberta, we're a software engineering agency focused on building custom solutions that actually solve problems—not generic products that force you to adapt your business to their limitations.

Mission

To make enterprise-grade software systems accessible, affordable, and impactful for businesses of all sizes, while contributing to the advancement of technology and strengthening local communities.

Vision

To evolve from a local software agency into a globally recognized leader in research, development, and innovation—shaping the future of technology and its integration into society.

Core Values

Accessibility of Technology

Enterprise tools should not be restricted by cost or complexity.

Community Advancement

Technology should directly improve local economies and quality of life.

Engineering Excellence

Solutions must be robust, scalable, and maintainable.

Future-Focused Innovation

Continuous learning and adaptation to emerging technologies.

The Founder

Anthony Tyler-Scott Dinunzio is a self-developed software engineer with deep roots in technology and systems design. From early experiments with game modding and server hosting to leading complex engineering projects in the oil & gas sector, Anthony brings real-world problem-solving experience to every project.

FutureVisionMachines represents more than a business—it's a long-term commitment to building tools that matter and contributing to how technology shapes our world.
