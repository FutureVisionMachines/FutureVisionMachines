Cookie Policy

How we use cookies

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide a better user experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function (e.g., session management)
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until deleted or expired

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies (e.g., analytics providers). These are governed by the respective third party's privacy policy.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.
